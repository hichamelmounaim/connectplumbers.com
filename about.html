About ConnectPlumbers

Practical plumbing knowledge for every homeowner.

Our Mission

ConnectPlumbers was created for one reason: plumbing should not be a mystery. Too many homeowners panic at the first sign of a leak, overpay for simple repairs, or make costly mistakes because they did not have access to clear, honest information. We are here to change that.

We publish detailed, step-by-step guides written in plain language — no plumber jargon, no condescending tone. Whether you are fixing a dripping faucet for the first time or maintaining your water heater like a pro, ConnectPlumbers has you covered.

What We Offer

  • DIY Repair Guides: Step-by-step instructions for the most common plumbing problems, complete with tool lists and time estimates.
  • Maintenance Tips: Preventative advice to help you avoid expensive emergency calls.
  • Free Tools: Our Water Usage Calculator and Pipe Size Estimator help you plan and save.
  • Professional Advice: Honest guidance on when a job is beyond DIY and you should call a licensed plumber.

Who We Are

We are a team of home improvement writers and former tradespeople who believe in empowering homeowners. Our content is researched, reviewed for accuracy, and designed to save you time and money. We do not sell plumbing services — we give you the knowledge to make informed decisions.

Our Promise

Every guide on ConnectPlumbers is free, ad-supported, and written with your safety in mind. We always tell you when a job requires a professional. We never recommend shortcuts that could cause damage or violate building codes.
